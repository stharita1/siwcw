Who are we?

Society Initiated for Women and Children Welfare SIWCW is a non-government, non-profitable organization working for women and children welfare in Nepal to promote womenâ€™s status, empowering and enhancing women and children's livelihood opportunities.The organization is led by women only and is functioning on a national level. SIWCW comprises of 25 women member and its executive board is governed by women. Since the organization is focused on girls and women we provide membership to women who are interested and dedicated to work in women and children welfare. SIWCW is a national level organization, we are currently working on four districts of Nepal:Jhapa, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur and Kathmandu.Alongside, we run different capacity building programs and awareness campaigns covering the entire nation.
