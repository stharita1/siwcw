About Us

SIWCW is a non-government, non-profitable, grassroot, women led organization based on Kathmandu, working for women and children welfare. It strives to promote women’s status, empowering and enhancing women and children's livelihood opportunities.

SIWCW'S Objectives

  • To organize different programs for social, academic, economic and cultural welfare of women and children
  • To support in women empowerment through different income generation and capacity building training
  • To advocate for protection and promotion of women and children
  • To organize different programs for welfare of marginalized, back ward and minority groups of women and children
  • To organize different awareness raising program on various burning issues of society
  • To make efforts for women independence and empowerment
  • To organize different programs for protection and promotion of the human rights of women and children
  • To boost youth participation in the utilization of natural resources and enhance their skills and knowledge
