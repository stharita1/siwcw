Income Generation Program


Vocational Training and Income Generation

With an aim to encourage women in entrepreneurship and enhance employment prospects, we provide different Vocational Skills Training to teach and enhance the skillset for girls and young women who are unemployed, deprived of opportunities,exploited or at risk of exploitation. We facilitate them with necessary trainings, skills and opportunities to help them sustain their livelihoods which will be instrumental in breaking out of the vicious cycle of poverty and in reducing youth unemployment. After training, we connects them to the market, sale their products and facilitates them for marketing skill. Eventually, it supports them for Income Generation and Economic Empowerment of women and girls
